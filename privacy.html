Privacy Policy for Kindred Nexus Engine

Effective Date: 2026-02-17

The Kindred Nexus Engine is an internal automation tool used solely by Kindred Archive to manage and publish owned content to Pinterest.

Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or share external user data. The app only accesses the owner's account to upload images (Pins) and retrieve performance metrics (Analytics).

For questions, contact the repository owner via GitHub.
